GNOME Foundation Organization

Board of Directors

The Board of Directors is elected by the Membership to carry out much of the GNOME Foundation's tasks.

Advisory Board

The Advisory Board helps the Board of Directors determine the overall direction for the GNOME Foundation and GNOME.

Executive Director

The Executive Director works on many different aspects of the GNOME Foundation's tasks, from fundraising and being a liason with other organizations, to helping to determine areas where GNOME needs improvement and finding new projects within GNOME and the GNOME Foundation.
